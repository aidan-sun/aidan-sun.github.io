ABOUT

Hi, I’m Aidan Sun! This is all placeholder text and images right now! I will probably add my qualifications and skills to this page, too :). Originating from Suwanee, Georgia, I grew up with a love for technology. I’ve always had a keen eye for design, and was introduced to UX Design by a family friend. Since then, I’ve wanted to pursue working in the field, with a goal of improving much of the janky and poorly designed interfaces people have to work with every day. I also have experiences in some computer related fields, being well versed in HTML and CSS, as well as being a swift and eager learner, hoping to expand my horizons. I am a former Boy Scout and swimmer, gaining valuable experience in leadership from both hobbies. In my freetime, I enjoys listening to and making music.
