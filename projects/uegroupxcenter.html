CO-DESIGN & TELEHEALTH

TELEHEALTH & CAREGIVERS

An exploration into the telehealth space, creating a mobile, at-home experience for caregivers of their elderly loved ones. This project had a unique focus of co-designs to facilitate our research.

    UX Design + Research

    Mobile App & UI Design

    Ecosystem Exploration

    Team Project

    Spring 2022

OVERVIEW

Project Statement

The current at-home healthcare experience is one that is fragmented and difficult to navigate, especially for caregivers. Our team set out to design medium to high-fidelity prototypes of an at-home mobile telehealth experience to assist those who are caregivers for their elderly loved ones.

User Group

Middle-class solo travelers that can afford to live sustainably, but have neutral attitudes towards sustainable policies & movements.

Approach

Because sustainability is such a complex problem space, our team divided ourselves into two subprojects for flight (expedia.com) and lodging (vrbo.com) in order to better tackle unique sustainability issues related to each. We utilized an agile approach, implementing two-week sprints for each subproject.
