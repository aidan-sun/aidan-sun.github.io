OVERVIEW

Internship

I interned under a senior UX designer at Yunex Traffic over the summer, assisting with research and design. Many of my contributions were in user research, conducting surveys, collaborating with engineers, etc. as well as helping design the software.

Project Structure

For much of the project I worked on, I was assisting the senior UX designer with research into our user group and current customers, helping with surveys and interviews, doing a competetive & comparative analysis, creating user personas, helping create user flows and sitemaps, and more. I also assisted in the design process, especially when it came to sketching and low-fidelity designs of the software. I learned a lot about the interaction between software and hardware during this project.
